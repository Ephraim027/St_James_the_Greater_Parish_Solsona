Ephraim B. Espiritu

Lay Eucharistic Minister (LEM)

By creating this digital information display, I was inspired by St. Carlo Acutis, whose deep devotion to Jesus Christ in the Holy Eucharist led him to use technology to share Eucharistic miracles throughout the world. His faith inspired me to offer my own knowledge and skills in service to the Church.

This project is also my personal act of thanksgiving to God. For many years, I prayed for opportunities to learn and grow beyond what I had learned in college. Through God's providence and blessings, He answered those prayers by opening doors to online learning, that helped me develop my knowledge and skills.

Through this digital information display, I am able to apply what I have learned from my online studies by creating a resource that serves the parish, guides visitors and pilgrims.

Through the intercession of St. Carlo Acutis, I ask for his prayers that I may continue to use my knowledge and skills faithfully, always use technology for the greater glory of God, deepen my relationship with God, remain faithful in prayer, and continue to grow in holiness through Christ.

St. James the Greater Parish - Solsona
